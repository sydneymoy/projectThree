Welcome to the Happy Paws Adoption Festival!

Join us for the Happy Paws Festival! Celebrate dog adoption with fun activities, meet-and-greets, live music, and more. Together, we can help every dog find a loving home!

Get your tickets today and help support dog adoption! Check out our Events page for more information.

DAY 1 - DEC 8th
(8:00AM - 9:20AM)

RESCUE RUN

OPENING EVENT - RESCUE RUN
8:00 AM - Check-in opens for runners, families, and their dogs, with a warm-up area and music playing.
8:45 AM - Kickoff speech.
9:00 AM - Start the 5K race.
9:20 AM - Start the 1-mile family fun run.
CLOSING EVENT - Awards will be presented for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place. The festival will conclude with live music, street food vendors, exciting giveaways and raffles, followed by a thank-you speech encouraging attendees to continue supporting local shelters and explore volunteer opportunities. Donation booths will be set up.

DAY 2 - DEC 9th
(12:00PM - 4:00PM)

HAPPY PAWS MARKET VENDOR

OPENING EVENT - HAPPY PAWS MARKET VENDOR
12:00 PM - 4:00 PM
Pet lovers can explore a variety of local vendors offering unique dog products, from nutritious treats and toys to stylish accessories.

MIDDAY EVENT- HAPPY PAWS PHOTOSHOOT
4:00PM - 5:30PM
Free photo shoot for families and their pets, featuring fun backdrops and the option to receive shareable digital copies of their photos. Attendees can also enjoy local brews from a beer vendor on site.

(12:00PM - 4:00PM)

EAST BAY LOCAL RESCUES, ADOPTION MEET & GREET
2:30PM - 4:00PM
