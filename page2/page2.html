DAY 1 - DEC 8th
(8:00AM - 9:20AM)

RESUCE RUN

8:00 AM - Check-in opens for runners, families, and their dogs, with a warm-up area and music playing.
8:45 AM - Kickoff speech.
9:00 AM - Start the 5K race.
9:20 AM - Start the 1-mile family fun run.
Ending Event - Awards will be presented for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place. The festival will conclude with live music, street food vendors, exciting giveaways and raffles, followed by a thank-you speech encouraging attendees to continue supporting local shelters and explore volunteer opportunities. Donation booths will be set up.

DAY 2 - DEC 9th
(12:00PM - 4:00PM)

HAPPY PAWS MARKET VENDOR

Opening Event - Happy Paws Market Vendor
12:00 PM - 4:00 PM
Pet lovers can explore a variety of local vendors offering unique dog products, from nutritious treats and toys to stylish accessories.

Midday Event - Happy Paws Photo Shoot
4:00PM - 5:30PM
Free photo shoot for families and their pets, featuring fun backdrops and the option to receive shareable digital copies of their photos. Attendees can also enjoy local brews from a beer vendor on site.

DAY 2 - DEC 9th
(12:00PM - 4:00PM)

ADOPT A PET

EAST BAY LOCAL RESCUES, ADOPTION MEET & GREET
2:30PM - 4:00PM
Come join East Bay rescues to meet adoptable pets, learn about the adoption process, and get information on volunteer opportunities with local rescue organizations. Drinks and snacks will be provided.
